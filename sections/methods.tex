\section{Methods}
\label{sec:methods}

\subsection{Environment}

We implement three gridworld environments of increasing complexity:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{CoopNav-2}: Two agents navigate to shared goal positions.
  \item \textbf{CoopNav-4}: Four agents with overlapping observation spaces.
  \item \textbf{ResourceAlloc}: Agents must divide limited resources optimally.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Training}

All agents use Proximal Policy Optimisation (PPO) with shared parameters.
Hyperparameters are listed in Table~\ref{tab:hyperparams}.

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\hline
\textbf{Parameter} & \textbf{Value} \\
\hline
Learning rate      & $3 \times 10^{-4}$ \\
Discount ($\gamma$)& 0.99 \\
Clip ratio         & 0.2 \\
Batch size         & 2048 \\
Epochs per update  & 10 \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\caption{PPO hyperparameters used across all experiments.}
\label{tab:hyperparams}
\end{table}

\subsection{Evaluation}

We evaluate each configuration over 5 independent seeds and report mean $\pm$ std.
See \texttt{data/results.csv} for raw numbers.
